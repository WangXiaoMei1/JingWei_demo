Dance Generation and Control of Humanoid Robot Over Online Music
Project
Dance Robot

    • In this project, we extract 3D pose of solo dancers from dance videos we collected from the internet and cut them into primitive units w.r.t. the beat sequence of its background music;
    • We generate improvisational dance with unit selection methodology based on two criterion:
      • Symantic relevance of dance units;
      • Stability of the robot when it transitions from one unit to another.
    • The chosen units are mapped to Nao's joint space and a PID controller is applied to synchronize Nao's dance with online music stimulation.

Look&Listen: Mutual Attention between Visual and Auditory Sensory
Project
Look and Listen

    • We focus on mining the inter-relationship between the audio flow and the image flow of videos, and test the capability of our model on the Video Action Recognition task;
    • For each sample of image frame, we sample the 1s audio clip centered by the image and apply STFT to obtain the Mel-spectrogram;
    • We model the relationship between the image flow and the audio flow (in Mel-spectrom) with scaled dot-production attention, the result of which is processed with Psedo-3D network for classification
    • Higher classification accuracy is expected.

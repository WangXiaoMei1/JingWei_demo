Real-Time Music-Driven Dance Generation for Humanoid Robot

“Dancing robot” seems to be a contradictory term itself. On the one hand, the word “robot” leaves the expression of cold machine doing dull and highly repetitive tasks with no emotion, variation, or interaction. On the other hand, “dance” is a form of performing art which expresses feelings, delivers creativity, and interacts with the music. Yet, the emergence of humanoid robots and pursuit of artificial intelligence have led to the aspiration of equipping robots with human capabilities to feel, think, and act. Dancing Robot, therefore, has found its consistency in both the robotics and the art field.

With this project, we aim to explore the term "creativity" at the intersection between robotics and music, e.g., the dance generation process.
